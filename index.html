Trip Description

Title Roan Highlands
Location Roan Mountain
Tennessee & North Carolina Border
Type Camping & Hiking
Dificulty Moderate
Season Spring & Summer
Length 3 days, 2 Nights
9 Mile Out and Back
Description This was one of the most fun hikes accompanied with some of the most beautiful campsites I have had the pleasure of sleeping in. It is the perfect backpacking trip for a weekend getaway. I arrived at the carvers gap entracnce to the Appalachian Trail around 4:00 on Friday afternoon. This allowed plenty of time to climb the 1.5 miles to Roan High Knob and set up camp. It's a nice shaded hike up with low-grade switchbacks. The next morning I backtracked down from Roan High Knob and contiued on the AT for another 3 miles over the three largest balds on the enire trail, offering unobstructed 360 degree views for the entire day (don't forget sunscreen). We set up camp at the top of th final bald, Grassy Ridge Bald. There are tons of spots to pitch a tent all around the bald, it's best to try and grab one that is east facing so you can have watch the sunrise from your sleeping bag. Hand's down the best sunset and sunrise I have seen in my life. The next morning is a short 3 miles back to the parking area.
